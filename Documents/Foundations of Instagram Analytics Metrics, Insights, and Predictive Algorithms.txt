1. Purpose of This Document

This document explains:

What Instagram analytics tools provide today

How they convert raw user data into insights

It is written to help product, data, and strategy teams understand the logic behind analytics, not just the UI.

2. Core Analytics Pipeline (All Tools)

Almost all Instagram analytics tools follow this flow:

Collect raw metrics (views, likes, comments, followers)

Store snapshots over time

Group and compare similar content

Generate descriptive insights

(Advanced tools) Apply predictive or recommendation algorithms
